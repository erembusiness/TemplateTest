Sustainability Initiatives
Mixed-Plastic-Waste Pyrolysis Unit

Successfully completed mechanical construction in Geleen, Netherlands. First commercial-scale advanced recycling facility in partnership with Plastic Energy.

Certified Circular Polypropylene (PP)

Production at Saudi-based manufacturing site. TRUCIRCLE™ portfolio accredited under International Sustainability and Carbon Certification (ISCC).

Electrically Heated Steam Cracking

World's first demonstration plant launched in partnership with BASF and Linde. Potential to reduce CO₂ emissions by at least 90% compared to traditional methods.

Low-Carbon Methanol

Introduced certified low-carbon methanol product using carbon capture and utilization (CCU) technology to repurpose CO₂ from upstream processes.
